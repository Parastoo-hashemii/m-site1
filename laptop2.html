Product Description

High-quality Audio and Visuals

Designed with a bright and vivid display, the ASUS Vivobook Pro 14 OLED Laptop features true-to-life colours. The eye-catching 35.56 cm (14) NanoEdge 2.8K OLED display along with high-quality Harman Kardon audio offers an engaging experience when you use this laptop for work or play.

Superior Performance

Integrated with an AMD Ryzen Processor with an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3050 graphics card, up to 16 GB memory, and a 1 TB of PCIe SSD storage, this laptop delivers optimal performance, offering you the freedom and flexibility to multitask with ease and efficiency.

High-grade Display

The 16:10 aspect-ratio display of this laptop offers an immersive viewing experience. Its 2.8K OLED NanoEdge display offers brightness levels of up to 600 nits for clear visuals. And, its 84% screen-to-body ratio ensures a widescreen view. Moreover, with its 100% DCI-P3 colour gamut for bright colours, this laptop lets you enjoy life-like hues on the screen. The high-grade colour accuracy and low blue-light emissions make this laptop an ideal choice.
